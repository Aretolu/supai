Dining and drinks

Explore our resturantfor traditional Nigeria, oriental, Mongolian, and Mediterranean cuisines. Fulani Pool Bar offers barbecus, our piano lounge serves light snacks, and he pastry shop stocks fresh baking.

Bukka Resturant

Enjoy traditional Nigerian cuisine at the Transcorp Hilton Abuja hotel. Join business guests and locals as you feast on continental favorites from the buffet. African-style dark woods blend with light walls as natural light streams in at this Abuja restaurant, available for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Capital Bar

Unwind with colleagues or friends at this contemporary sports bar with low lighting at the Transcorp Hiton Abuja hotel. Leisure and business guests and fashionable young locals enjoy live music at this Abuja bar. Snack on a spring roll, chicken wings or a burger.

Fulani Pool Bar

With its traditional thatched roofing, mats and statues, this Abuja bar's design reflects the culture of Nigeria's Fulani people. Order barbecue à la carte specialties for your lunch or dinner. Unwind with a snack as you take in views of the Transcorp Hilton Abuja hotel's beautifully landscaped gardens.

Pastry Shop

Take a break from your busy schedule and relax with a tea or coffee and a French pastry in this Abuja café at the Transcorp Hilton Abuja hotel. Meat pies, croissants, cookies, cakes, oven-fresh bread, sandwiches and sweets are available to eat in or take away.

Piano Lounge

Hold an informal business meeting in the avant garde design lounge area of this Abuja bar, conveniently located by the Transcorp Hilton Abuja hotel's Lobby. Snack on chicken wings, burgers or sandwiches and sit back and enjoy the live piano music throughout the evening.

Zuma Resturant

Ambient low lighting forms the backdrop to romantic dining at this Abuja restaurant overlooking the water fountain. Choose from the à la carte menu of Mediterranean favorites and light tasty dishes. Dine with locals and business and leisure guests at the Transcorp Hilton Abuja hotel.
